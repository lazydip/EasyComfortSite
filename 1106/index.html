Check-in instructions with a car:

If you have a car and need parking, start by locating the parking entrance behind the Alura/Nuera buildings at 14 SE. As you approach the area, the parking lot entrance should be easy to spot. For directions, refer to the Google Maps display next to this information.

To assist in finding the parking entrance, a drawing with arrows indicating the surrounding area of the building and a photo highlighting the exact entrance location have been provided. Please refer to these visuals next to this text for guidance.

Please proceed to the garage gate and dial the number 2173. The team will open the garage gate for you remotely.

Once inside the condo's spiral parking structure, always stay close to the wall on your right side. As you descend, consistently take the first available right turn without skipping any. This path will guide you correctly through the spiral. Be attentive to parking spot numbers as you navigate, and you'll soon find your designated spot. If uncertain or lost, continue along the spiral, and you'll eventually locate your spot or a place to safely recalibrate your direction.

Every Vehicle must use Remote or Intercom Panel Before Entering parkade. Make sure door is closing behind you, before leaving the door. If vehicle is in front, keep some space, let the door close before proceeding.

If elevator button 11 is not working:

Sometimes there is an issue when the elevator button for the 11th floor cannot be pushed. This might happen if the entrance timeout has expired or someone has already arrived on the 11th floor.

In this case, go to the lobby - the button for this floor will always work from the garage. The intercom is located in the entrance area, situated between the front door and the inside door. Approach the intercom and dial A1106.

The entrance door will be opened remotely. Call the elevator and press the 11th floor. The door will be unlocked.

Use the black garage door opener and press Button 1 for entry. The remote control can be used both to tap in the elevator and to enter the building.

Once you have the fob, you can access the building, your floor, lobby, and garage within the building.
